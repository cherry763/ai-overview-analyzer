    To download your e-Aadhaar, you can visit the UIDAI MyAadhaar portal (https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/genricDownloadAadhaar/en), use the mAadhaar app, or the DigiLocker app, using your Aadhaar number, enrollment ID, or Virtual ID, and verifying with an OTP sent to your registered mobile number. 
    Here's a more detailed breakdown:
    1. UIDAI MyAadhaar Portal:
    • Visit the website: Go to https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/genricDownloadAadhaar/en. 
    • Choose your method: You can download using your Aadhaar number, enrollment ID, or Virtual ID (VID). 
    • Enter details: Provide the required information and security code. 
    • Generate OTP: An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number. 
    • Verify and download: Enter the OTP and click "Verify and Download". 
    2. mAadhaar App:
    • Install the app: Download the mAadhaar app from the Google Play Store or App Store.
    • Sign up/Log in: Register or sign in to the app.
    • Download Aadhaar: Navigate to "My Aadhaar" and click "Download Aadhaar".
    • Authenticate: Follow the prompts to authenticate yourself, usually via OTP. 
    3. DigiLocker:
    • Download the app: Download the DigiLocker app.
    • Sign in: Sign in to your DigiLocker account.
    • Link Aadhaar: If you haven't already, link your Aadhaar with your DigiLocker account.
    • Find and download: You can find and download your Aadhaar in the "Issued Documents" section. 
    Important Notes:
    • Password for e-Aadhaar: The password for your e-Aadhaar PDF is the first four letters of your name in capital letters followed by your year of birth (YYYY). 
    • Registered Mobile Number: You'll need a registered mobile number to receive the OTP for downloading. 
    • Alternative Methods: You can also download your e-Aadhaar using the UMANG app. 
    • Keep Aadhaar Updated: Ensure your Aadhaar number is updated with your mobile number and email address for accessing various Aadhaar-related services.
