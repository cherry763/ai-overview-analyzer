    Here's a more detailed breakdown:
    1. GoFirst (formerly GoAir):
    • What it was:
      An Indian low-cost airline, founded in 2005 and owned by the Wadia Group, based in Mumbai. 
    • What it offered:
      Flights to 9 international destinations and 27 domestic locations in India. 
    • Fleet:
      Operated an all Airbus A320 fleet. 
    • Services:
      Offered economy and business class tickets, pre-reservation of meals, and frequent flyer programs. 
    • Current Status:
      The airline ceased operations in May 2024, and the DGCA initiated the deregistration process. 
    2. Go! (airline):
    • What it was: A regional brand of Mesa Airlines, operating inter-island services within Hawaii.
    • What it offered: Flights within Hawaii.
    • Current Status: Ceased operations in Hawaii on April 1, 2014.
