    How to check PNR status of flight?
    Can't generate an AI overview right now. Try again later.An AI Overview is not available for this search
    AI Overview
    To check your flight's PNR (Passenger Name Record) status, visit the airline's website or app, look for a "Flight Status" or "Check Status" option, and enter your PNR number, flight number, or route details. 
    Here's a more detailed breakdown:
    • Find Your PNR:
      • Check your confirmation email from the airline, which should include your PNR. 
      • If you have an account with the airline, log in and find your flight details, where the PNR should be listed. 
      • If you can't find it, contact the airline's customer service for assistance. 
    • Check Flight Status Online:
      • Go to the airline's website or app. 
      • Look for a section labeled "Flight Status," "Check Status," or similar. 
      • Enter your PNR number, flight number, or route details as prompted. 
      • You can also use the airline's flight tracker to check the live status of your flight. 
    • Examples of Airlines with Flight Status Check:
      • Air India: Visit their website and enter your booking reference number or flight details to check flight status. 
      • IndiGo: Use their flight tracker to check the live status of domestic and international flights. 
      • American Airlines: You can check your flight status on their website or app. 
      • Lufthansa: You can also check your flight status on their website. 
    • Other Options:
      • Some airlines allow you to check flight status by sending an SMS with your PNR number. 
      • You can also use travel websites like MakeMyTrip or Cleartrip to check flight status.
